Sandwiches typically made with cheese:
Grilled Cheese Sandwich
  • A classic comfort food made with slices of cheese (usually cheddar, American, or Swiss) between two slices of buttered and toasted bread.
BLT Sandwich
  • A bacon, lettuce, and tomato sandwich that often includes a slice of cheese, such as American or Swiss, to add creaminess and flavor.
Philly Cheesesteak
  • A famous sandwich featuring thinly sliced beefsteak, often with sautéed onions and bell peppers, served on a long roll and topped with melted cheese, typically provolone or American.
Caprese Sandwich
  • A light and refreshing sandwich made with fresh mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, basil leaves, and drizzled with balsamic glaze or vinaigrette, usually served on a baguette or ciabatta bread.
Reuben Sandwich
  • A deli classic that includes corned beef, sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, and Russian or Thousand Island dressing, grilled between slices of rye bread.
