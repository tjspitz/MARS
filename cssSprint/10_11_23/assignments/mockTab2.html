Sandwiches typically made with Rye bread:
Reuben Sandwich:
  • A classic sandwich made with corned beef, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Russian dressing on rye bread.
Pastrami on Rye:
  • Thinly sliced pastrami piled high on rye bread with mustard and pickles.
Turkey and Swiss:
  • Slices of roasted turkey, Swiss cheese, lettuce, and a spread like mayonnaise or mustard on rye.
Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese:
  • Smoked salmon, cream cheese, red onion, capers, and dill on rye bread, often known as a "lox and bagel" style sandwich.
Tuna Salad:
  • Tuna salad made with canned tuna, mayonnaise, celery, and spices, served on rye bread with lettuce and tomato.
Roast Beef and Horseradish:
  • Thinly sliced roast beef with horseradish sauce, red onion, and lettuce on rye bread.
Turkey Reuben:
  • A variation of the classic Reuben, made with turkey instead of corned beef.
Veggie Delight:
  • A vegetarian sandwich with avocado, cucumber, lettuce, tomato, red onion, and sprouts on rye bread, often with a creamy dressing.
Egg Salad:
  • Hard-boiled eggs mashed with mayonnaise and seasonings, served on rye bread with lettuce or sprouts.
Ham and Swiss:
  • Slices of ham and Swiss cheese with mustard or honey mustard on rye.
